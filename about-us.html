ABOUT US

Welcome to Nanay's Food House, a family-run restaurant dedicated to bringing the heart and soul of Filipino cuisine to your table. Our menu features traditional dishes like sisig, chicharong bulaklak, and chicken inasal, all crafted with fresh, high-quality ingredients to deliver an authentic taste experience. Whether you're here for a family gathering, a friendly get-together, or a quiet meal, our cozy atmosphere and warm hospitality ensure every guest feels like part of our family. Thank you for choosing Nanay's Food House; we look forward to sharing our love for Filipino food with you.
