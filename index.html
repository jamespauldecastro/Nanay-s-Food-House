SIZZLING

Sizzling Liempo

Grilled liempo is a popular Filipino dish featuring succulent pork belly marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and soy sauce, then grilled to crispy perfection.

Sizzling Chicken

Sizzling Chicken is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, lemon or calamansi juice, garlic, and other seasonings before being seared on the hot plate.

Sizzling Pusit

Sizzling Pusit is marinated in a mixture of calamansi (Filipino lime), garlic, soy sauce, and other seasonings before being placed on the sizzling hot surface.

Sizzling Sisig

Sizzling Sisig is a savory and smoky mixture of minced pork, liver, and other ingredients served on a sizzling hot metal plate.

DISHES WITH EGG

shanghai silog

Shanghai Silog is a classic Filipino breakfast dish that combines the flavors of Chinese-inspired fried rice with a variety of Filipino-style protein toppings.

spam silog

Spam Silog is a classic Filipino breakfast dish that features the beloved canned meat product, Spam, as the protein component.

tocino silog

Tocino Silog is a classic Filipino breakfast dish that features tocino, a type of cured and sweetened pork, as the star ingredient.

tapsilog

Tapsilog is a classic Filipino breakfast dish that combines three key components: tapa (cured, dried meat), sinangag (garlic fried rice), and itlog (fried egg).

DRINKS

BLUE LEMONADE

Blue Lemonade is primarily tart and sweet, just like a traditional lemon-based lemonade. The lemon flavor is the dominant taste, providing a bright, citrusy note.

RED ICED TEA

Red iced tea has a bold, bright red or reddish-pink hue, it is very refreshing and thirst-quenching, with the tartness providing a nice contrast to the sweetness.

CUCUMBER

Cucumber Juice is crisp, clean, and hydrating. It's often described as tasting like a more concentrated version of biting into a fresh cucumber.

SOFT DRINKS

Soft Drinks deliver a sweet, fizzy, and refreshing sipping experience. The combination of sweetness, carbonation, and often-chilled temperature creates a very refreshing drinking experience.
